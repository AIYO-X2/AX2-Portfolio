The Virtual Parking Lot

A dystopian 3D art piece that speaks about the growing concern of a future where people have their wake controlled and encaged in virtual reality by political and economical organizations. Users have their bodies parked in what is left of the desolate earth, and their minds parked in a virtual utopia.
